FAQ

Common questions

Where does the data come from?
Telangana's Registration Department (IGR) and Sub-Registrar offices — the same records your legal team reviews during due diligence, normalized and made queryable for the first time. We process data from 7+ Hyderabad-area sub-registrar offices.
Is this just MagicBricks / 99acres data?
We aggregate listing-price data across MagicBricks, 99acres, and other portals — but we normalize it across 69 Hyderabad micromarkets, filter outliers, and compute weekly median ₹/sqft by zone and configuration. That is different from checking one portal at a time. Sub-registrar transaction data (registered sale prices) is on our roadmap and will be added in a future sprint.
How current is the data?
Weekly refresh on Starter and Growth tiers. Data is typically 7–14 days behind the registration date. Daily refresh available on Team tier.
We already use PropEquity or CRE Matrix. Why switch?
Those are institutional tools built for pan-India fund clients. Hyderabad micromarket granularity is not their core focus.
